# 数术书库 / Shushu Book Collection

## 简介 / Introduction
This repository is a collection of Chinese traditional metaphysics (*shushu* 数术) classics, spanning the fields of Yi Jing studies, Liu Ren divination, Qi Men Dun Jia, fate calculation (BaZi), Feng Shui, physiognomy, and general divination. It contains over 200 classical texts ranging from the pre-Qin period to the modern era.

本仓库收录中国传统数术典籍，涵盖易学、六壬、奇门遁甲、命理、堪舆风水、相术、占卜等各个门类，共收录古籍两百余种，时间跨度自先秦至近代。
所有文件按类别分存于各子文件夹中，文件命名格式为：`书名-朝代-作者.txt`（部分作者不详者以"佚名"代替）。

All files are organized into subject-based subfolders. File names follow the pattern: `Title-Dynasty-Author.txt` (authors unknown are listed as *佚名* "anonymous").

## 分类说明 / Category Notes

- **周易**：收录《周易》原文及历代注疏，包括汉代易纬、宋明理学易注、象数图书等。
- **六壬**：大六壬为三式之一，以天干地支与十二神将推演吉凶，本类收录历代六壬要籍。
- **奇门遁甲**：三式之一，兼收太乙神数，为古代军事与日用数术重要分支。
- **命理**：以生辰八字推算人命运的学问，收录子平法、星命学等经典著作。
- **堪舆风水**：研究阴阳宅选址与布局的学问，收录形势派、理气派（玄空）等重要典籍。
- **相术**：通过观察人的形貌、气色等推断命运吉凶的学问，包括面相、骨相著作。
- **占卜**：包括钱币卦、梅花易数、禽星、测字等各种占卜方法。
- **其他**：五行理论、天文星占、军事数术及其他综合性著作。
- **現代**：近现代学者对传统数术的整理与研究著作。
- 
- **Yi Jing** (周易): The original *Book of Changes* and all generations of commentaries, including Han-dynasty *wei* apocrypha, Song-Ming Neo-Confucian annotations, and numerological diagrams.
- **Liu Ren** (六壬): One of the "Three Arts" (*san shi*), which uses Heavenly Stems, Earthly Branches, and twelve divine generals to forecast events.
- **Qi Men Dun Jia** (奇门遁甲): Another of the Three Arts; also includes Tai Yi Shen Shu. Historically important in military strategy and daily divination.
- **Fate Calculation** (命理): The art of reading destiny through the Four Pillars of Birth (BaZi). Includes Zi Ping method and star-fate schools.
- **Feng Shui** (堪舆风水): The study of site selection and layout for dwellings of the living and the dead, covering the Form School and the Compass/Qi School (Xuan Kong).
- **Physiognomy** (相术): Reading a person's destiny through physical features — face, bones, hands, and bearing.
- **Divination** (占卜): Various casting methods including coin hexagrams, Plum Blossom Numerology, bird-star divination, and word-analysis fortune-telling.
- **Miscellaneous** (其他): Five-Element theory texts, astronomical/astral divination, military strategy-numerology, and other cross-category works.
- **Modern** (現代): Works by modern scholars compiling and studying traditional metaphysics.

## 聲明 / Disclaimer
The texts in this repository are traditional Chinese classics, the vast majority of which are in the public domain. They are provided solely for academic research and cultural preservation. 
本仓库所收录的文献均为中国传统古籍，绝大多数已属公共领域。整理、上传仅供学术研究与文化传承之用。
