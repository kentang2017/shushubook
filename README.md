# 數術書庫 / Shushu Book Collection

## 簡介 / Introduction
This repository is a collection of Chinese traditional metaphysics (*shushu* 數術) classics, spanning the fields of Yi Jing studies, Liu Ren divination, Qi Men Dun Jia, fate calculation (BaZi), Feng Shui, physi​​annoi, class 0. ranging from the pre-Qin period to the modern era.

本倉庫收錄中國傳統數術典籍，涵蓋易學、六壬、奇門遁甲、命理、堪輿風水、相術、占卜等各個門類，共收錄古籍兩百餘種，時間跨度自先秦至近代。
所有檔案依類別分存於各子資料夾中，檔案命名格式為：`書名-朝代-作者.txt`（部分作者不詳以"佚名"取代）。

All files are organized into subject-based subfolders. File names follow the pattern: `Title-Dynasty-Author.txt` (authors unknown are listed as *佚名* "anonymous").

## 分類說明 / Category Notes

- **周易**：收錄《周易》原文及歷代注疏，包括漢代易緯、宋明理學易註、象數圖書等。
- **六壬**：大六壬為三式之一，以天干地支與十二神將推演吉兇，本類收錄歷代六壬要籍。
- **奇門遁甲**：三式之一，兼收太乙神數，為古代軍事與日用數術重要分支。
- **命理**：以生辰八字推算人命運的學問，收錄子平法、星命學等經典著作。
- **堪輿風水**：研究陰陽宅選址與版面的學問，收錄形勢派、理氣派（玄空）等重要典籍。
- **相術**：透過觀察人的形貌、氣色等推論命運吉凶的學問，包括面相、骨相著作。
- **占卜**：包括錢幣卦、梅花易數、禽星、測字等各種占卜方法。
- **其他**：五行理論、天文星佔、軍事數術及其他綜合性著作。
- **現代**：近現代學者對傳統數術的整理與研究著作。
- **Yi Jing** (周易): The original *Book of Changes* and all generations of commentaries, including Han-dynasty *wei* apocrypha, Song-Ming Neo-Confucian annotations, and numerological diagrams.
- **Liu Ren** (六壬): One of the "Three Arts" (*san shi*), which uses Heavenly Stems, Earthly Branches, and twelve divine generals to forecast events.
- **Qi Men Dun Jia** (奇門遁甲): Another of the Three Arts; also includes Tai Yi Shen Shu. Historically important in military strategy and daily divination.
- **Fate Calculation** (命理): The art of reading destiny through the Four Pillars of Birth (BaZi). Includes Zi Ping method and star-fate schools.
- **Feng Shui** (堪輿風水): The study of site selection and layout for dwellings of the living and the dead, covering the Form School and the Compass/Qi School (Xuan Kong).
- **Physiognomy** (相術): Reading a person's destiny through physical features — face, bones, hands, and bearing.
- **Divination** (占卜): Various casting methods including coin hexagrams, Plum Blossom Numerology, bird-star divination, and word-analysis fortune-telling.
- **Miscellaneous** (其他): Five-Element theory texts, astronomical/astral divination, military strategy-numerology, and other cross-category works.
- **Modern** (現代): Works by modern scholars compiling and studying traditional metaphysics.

## 聲明 / Disclaimer
The texts in this repository are traditional Chinese classics, the vast majority of which are in the public domain. They are provided solely for academic research and cultural preservation.
本倉庫所收錄的文獻均為中國傳統古籍，絕大多數已屬公共領域。整理、上傳僅供學術研究與文化傳承之用。
